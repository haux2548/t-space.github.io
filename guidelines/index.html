Guidelines

Equipment

All users of equipment must be trained in the safety guidelines for the hardware that they use.

Soldering

  • The vacuums must be used over the soldering irons when they are in use to reduce the amount of air pollution.

Materials

Acrylic

  • Each student is allowed one sheet of acrylic per term and unlimited scrap acrylic
